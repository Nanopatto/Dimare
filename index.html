About Us

Di Mare Tailoring was born in Calabria, Italy, in 1948. In the aftermath of World War II, my family arrived in Costa Rica as refugees, bringing with them thread, needle, and the determination to begin anew.

While sewing military uniforms, they also wove a new home.

Today, this tailoring house is a tribute to that heritage, to the values that define me, and to the timeless elegance of the craft.
